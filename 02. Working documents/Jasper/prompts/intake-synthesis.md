# Intake & synthesis

**Role:** Intake & synthesis (see [`LOP-agent-workflow.md`](../LOP-agent-workflow.md)).

**Inputs:** Only rows **Y** from Step 0 manifest (repeat manifest header in your reply).

**Tasks:**

1. Summarize **client situation**, **what they are buying**, **why us** (win themes from **partner/gold** inputs only), **risks/constraints**.  
2. List **assumptions** — label each as *supported by source* vs *needs confirmation*.  
3. Emit **numbered clarifying questions** (one tight block).  
4. Flag **opposing inputs** (contradictions) with **Option A / Option B** wording — do **not** merge silently.

**Output:** One-page **problem statement** suitable for **Gate A**, plus assumptions + questions + conflict notes.

**Forbidden:** New client facts, fees, credentials, or disclaimers not in sources.
