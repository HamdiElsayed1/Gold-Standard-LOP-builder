A four-phase workplan turns the objection process into the launchpad for responsible AI at scale

1

Diagnose

  • Diagnose the objection process and its pain points.
  • Assess six use cases, data and controls.
  • Link findings to cycle time, errors and Awb deadlines.

Deliverable: diagnostic fact base

2

Design

  • Design target processes for bezwaar.
  • Define AI-use-case architecture.
  • Specify governance for auditable AI-agent controls.

Deliverable: target design and governance

3

Decide

  • Prepare business cases for priority use cases.
  • Prioritize choices for implementation.
  • Connect choices to service quality outcomes.

Deliverable: business cases and priorities

4

Mobilize

  • Convert decisions into mobilization plans.
  • Define operating model choices.
  • Build the roadmap for responsible AI at scale.

Deliverable: mobilization plan and roadmap

Source: RFP and partner answers; team analysis
