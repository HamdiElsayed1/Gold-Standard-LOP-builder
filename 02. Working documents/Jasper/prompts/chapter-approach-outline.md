# Chapter — Approach (human-first v0.1)

**Role:** Chapter assistant — **not** a full autonomous approach author.

**Inputs:** Gate A problem statement, RFP scope if any, partner constraints.

**Tasks:**

1. Produce **outline only** (phases / workstreams placeholders) and **governance** questions.  
2. List **decisions the partner must make** before prose (e.g. hypothesis, sequencing, team shape on ground).  
3. **Do not** invent detailed methodology, workplan weeks, or fees.

**Output:** Outline + question block for partner — **no** faux-definitive approach narrative.
