Slide 2 — Context and Objectives
2 McKinsey & Company

Belastingdienst must scale responsible AI now to relieve workload, preserve public value, and modernize legally constrained processes

The situation

  • Labour scarcity, ageing-driven uitstroom, and rising case complexity intensify pressure on the organization.
  • Regie op AI and the 2025 AI vision set three core values — geloofwaardig, zorgvuldig, verantwoord — across six AI pillars.
  • First deelopdracht is a full redesign of the generic bezwaarproces, from ontstaan geschil to acceptatie uitspraak, within Awb deadlines.
  • RFP IUC26-011 asks for all essential AI capabilities — infrastructure, skills, processes, legal frameworks, ethics — under a EUR 5 mln, 12-month contract.

Our day-one answer

We recommend a combined value, compliance, and operating-model agenda: redesign the generic bezwaarproces with AI agents inside Awb constraints, while building the infrastructure, governance, skills, and ethics capabilities that let the Belastingdienst scale AI responsibly across the six 2026 themes.

Without this integrated move, AI stays at pilot scale and the productivity, service-quality, and compliance gains the operating environment now demands remain out of reach.

Source: RFP IUC26-011 and Bijlage 2 Visie op AI; ContextDoc web search 2026; team analysis
