Slide 7 — Market Trends
7 McKinsey & Company

European banks are shifting from Copilot pilots to industrialised agentic SDLC as the EU AI Act and DORA close the regulatory runway

2024

ING Group disclosed enterprise rollout of generative AI across customer service and engineering

ING publicly deployed a generative-AI customer-service assistant in the Netherlands and cited AI as core to the 2025-2027 Group strategy.

2026 Q3

EU AI Act high-risk obligations apply from August 2026

Regulation 2024/1689 entered into force August 2024; GPAI obligations apply August 2025 and high-risk system obligations from August 2026.

IN FORCE

DORA applicable since January 2025, covering ICT third-party risk including AI tooling

Regulation 2022/2554 imposes ICT risk-management, incident-reporting and third-party controls on EU banks, explicitly covering cloud and AI tooling in critical functions.

What this means for ING BE

NBP scale-up rides an already-communicated Group AI agenda, not a fresh bet.

H2 2026 is the right window to lock in reuse governance before AI Act high-risk rules bite.

Bake DORA third-party and resilience controls into NBP reusable templates from day one.
