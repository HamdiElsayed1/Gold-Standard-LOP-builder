      Workplan · Workstreams

      Workstreams

      Four substantive streams (prep and readout sit inside weeks, not as separate streams).

      Each stream: name, objective, share of team time (illustrative), dependencies, definition of done.

      WS1 — Diagnostic & fact base
      ~25% core team time
      Objective: issue tree, interviews, internal/external fact base for decisions. Dependencies: data access TBD. Definition of done: agreed backlog and v1 fact pack. Kickoff prep sits in week 1.
      WS2 — Solution / initiative design
      ~35% core team time
      Objective: where-to-play, options, prioritization criteria, workshop-ready materials. Dependencies: WS1 outputs. Definition of done: shortlist and storyline for steering cadence.
      WS3 — Value & feasibility
      ~20% core team time
      Objective: directional economics and feasibility to support trade-offs. Dependencies: finance / market inputs TBD. Definition of done: ranges with explicit assumptions.
      WS4 — Integration & client moments
      ~20% core team time
      Objective: integrate streams, steerco prep, dry runs, final readout pack & next steps. Dependencies: partner review TBD. Definition of done: signed-off storyline and readout. Final readout sits in this stream, not a fifth stream.

      Week-by-week plan

      First row: milestones (including soft start and steering cadence). Following rows: workstreams.

      Legend: Client workshops, steering moments, data sources, and owners to be confirmed as the plan firms up.
