Slide 2 — Context and Objectives
2 McKinsey & Company

Belastingdienst must turn AI ambition into responsible scale before labour shortages and budget pressure erode service capacity

The situation

  • Assignment sits inside Regie op AI and the wider Digitale Transformatie program, anchored in the Meerjarenstrategie 2025-2030
  • Current AI governance is not mature enough to scale broadly: one coherent operating model, toetsingsproces and decision process is missing
  • Six use cases are in scope, starting with redesign of the bezwaarproces from ontstaan geschil to acceptatie uitspraak (15 March-1 August 2026)
  • Capabilities must be built while experienced staff exit and the 12-month budget is capped at EUR 5 mln incl. VAT

Our day-one answer

Treat this as a transformation and operating-model assignment, not another pilot: build the AI capabilities, governance and delivery model that let Belastingdienst deploy AI across core processes, and prove it by redesigning the bezwaarproces in the first six months.

Implication: without a coherent operating model and immediate delivery on use case one, efficiency gains stay unrealised, capacity pressure compounds as staff exit, and the window to modernise core public services closes under tightening legal and budget constraints.

Source: RFP IUC26-011 and Visie op AI bij de Belastingdienst; ContextDoc web search; team analysis

Slide 7 — Market Trends
7 McKinsey & Company

AI in Dutch government is shifting from experiment to production, but only sovereign, compliance-by-design solutions will scale

DATE NOT STATED

Public-sector AI moves from pilots to core-process infrastructure

Government organisations now treat AI as infrastructure for core processes, provided it lands within European and Dutch legal frameworks.

DATE NOT STATED

Sovereign and European AI gains ground via GPT-NL

Dutch policy attention shifts to sovereign, open-source or European-anchored AI such as GPT-NL to reduce dependence on non-European suppliers.

DATE NOT STATED

Generative AI and agents enter knowledge work and decision support

Generative AI and agentic applications accelerate into casework and decision support, directly relevant to bezwaar, inning, and fiscalist support.

DATE NOT STATED

Compliance-by-design becomes the public-sector default

Privacy, archiefvorming, uitlegbaarheid, and governance must be co-designed into AI solutions, not bolted on after deployment.

WHAT THIS MEANS FOR BELASTINGDIENST

Foreground sovereign, public-sector AI to match the client's stated dependency-reduction constraints.

Treat the bezwaar redesign as production-grade AI, not a pilot, to align with the shift from experiment to core process.

Design AI Act, AVG, Awb, and Archiefwet requirements into the solution from day one.

Use AI as a capacity strategy against arbeidsmarktkrapte and vergrijzing, not only an efficiency play.

Slide 8 — Approach
8 McKinsey & Company

A three-phase approach delivers the bezwaarproces redesign from 15 March to 1 August 2026 while building reusable AI capabilities in parallel

1

Diagnose (15 March - mid April 2026)

  • Map the bezwaarproces end-to-end, from ontstaan geschil to acceptatie uitspraak
  • Pressure-test against AI Act, AVG, Awb, and Archiefwet constraints with legal counterparts
  • Identify quick wins and prioritised AI agent opportunities in the primaire proces

DELIVERABLE

Analyserapport plus quick-wins backlog for the bezwaarproces

2

Design (mid April - mid June 2026)

  • Co-design the redesigned bezwaarproces with responsible AI agents embedded
  • Define the AI strategy for the use case against the six AI pillars
  • Build business cases sized to the EUR 5 mln, 12-month envelope

DELIVERABLE

Procesontwerp, AI-strategie, and business cases per subassignment

3

Prepare to implement (mid June - 1 August 2026)

  • Define and delimit follow-on subassignments with market-conform pricing
  • Stand up the toetsings- and besluitvormingsproces for production deployment
  • Hand over to Belastingdienst teams with mobilisation plan for next phases

DELIVERABLE

Implementatievoorbereiding plus subopdracht-afbakening en tariefkader

Parallel AI-capability build across the six pillars throughout all three phases

Compliance-by-design under AI Act, AVG, and Awb at every gate
